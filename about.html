About Me

I have over 30 Years of experience across diverse industry sectors covering Defence, Manufacturing, Automotive, Service & Banking Financial Systems. Apart from serving in Indian Air Force for close to 23 years, I also have the honour of being a member of Indian Air Force contingent on UN Peace Keeping Mission in Democratic Republic of Congo (MONUC).

Enterprise Transformation

Led Enterprise Transformations program for Central Functions group of Societe Generale group across India & Romania covering Agile@Scale, DevOps & Cloud transformation initiatives to build business agility levels across the organization.

An Ambassador for DevOps Agile Skills Association (DASA) in India to build DevOps & Agile Community through MeetUps to build awareness about Principles, Skills, Knowledge & Focus areas.

Payments Transformation

Led the biggest transformation program to set up 24x7 real time transaction capability for Institutional Business across Australia as part of New Payments Platform program driven by Reserve Bank of Australia using Scaling Agile Framework (Scrum of Scrums) with a budget of over 58Mn AUD.

Operational Process Transformation

Led transformation program to reduce the dependence on Customer Contact Centre through setting up a Business Process Management and Electronic Documentation Management platforms on PEGA & Filenet using Agile & traditional delivery models.

Infrastructure Transformation

Led Infrastructure transformation program for General Motors across 22 Countries as part of International Operations to modernize their infrastructure involving data centre consolidation, Remote site virtualization, domain consolidation, setting up new sites, drive new solution development through POC etc.

Led Digital Transformation program to set up MPLS based VOIP Network to digitize communication systems using state-of-the-art communication infrastructure, set up Satellite Communication Network designed for high reliability and redundancy.
