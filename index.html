Nivarti - A Quick Intro

Nivarti is an ICF Accredited Career & Leadership Transformation Coach, Service Veteran and an UN Peace Keeper with over 30 Years of experience across Industry sectors.

Nivarti believes agility is a state of mind, transformation never a destination but a continuous and an ongoing journey. A continuous unlearner, Nivarti believes unlearning is a significant ingredient to sustain transformation whether it is personal or organizational.

Nivarti promotes a culture of "being dispensable" through identifying right talent & building capability to create sustainability in organizations.
