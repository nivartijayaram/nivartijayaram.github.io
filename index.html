Nivarti - A Quick Intro

I am an expert at Enterprise Transformation covering people, product, process & technology helping them not just succeed but thrive in the VUCA world. I am passionate about people helping them in enhancing Self-awareness & Presence in people so they can identify and realize their full potential.

An ICF Accredited Leadership & Career Transformation Coach, Service Veteran and an UN Peacekeeper, I believe agility is a state of mind, transformation never a destination but a continuous and an ongoing journey. I promote a culture of "being dispensable" through identifying right talent & building capability to create sustainability in organizations.

I believe for everything that we learn newly in life, there’s something that needs to be unlearnt & my forthcoming book Unlearning – Your learning is never complete without it reflects my transformation journey.
